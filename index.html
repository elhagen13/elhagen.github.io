Introduction

Hi! I'm Ella Hagen, a second year computer science major!

At the moment, I'm planning on minoring in Computing for the Interactive Arts. I absolutely love all things art, so I'm hoping to combine both art and computer science as much as I can. I think that there's a lot of possibilities when it comes to the combination of the two, especially when it comes to web development. I hate the idea of missing out on something I could grow to be passionate about, so I love teaching myself and developing new skills.

There's a lot of amazing things about computer science, but in all honesty, it's a little intimidating how versatile it is. That's why I think it's important to try as many aspects of it to find your niche.
